## üöÄ Quick Options to Get a Short Link

### Option 1: Custom Domain (Best - Like fincke.dev) ‚≠ê

**Steps:**
1. **Buy a domain:**
   - `.dev` domain: [Google Domains](https://domains.google/) (~$12/year)
   - `.com` domain: [Namecheap](https://www.namecheap.com/) (~$10-15/year)
   - Search for: `yourname.dev` or `yourname.com`

2. **Add to Vercel:**
   - I've opened the Vercel domains page in your browser
   - Click "Add Domain"
   - Enter your domain (e.g., `yourname.dev`)
   - Copy the DNS records Vercel provides

3. **Configure DNS:**
   - Go to your domain registrar (where you bought the domain)
   - Add the DNS records Vercel provided
   - Wait 5-30 minutes for DNS to propagate

4. **Done!** Your portfolio will be live at `https://yourname.dev`

### Option 2: Free Link Shortener (Instant)

Use a free service to create a short link:

1. **bit.ly:**
   - Go to [bit.ly](https://bit.ly)
   - Create account
   - Shorten: `https://my-portfolio-jk1806-9fpr0ek6i.vercel.app`

2. **tinyurl.com:**
   - Go to [tinyurl.com](https://tinyurl.com)

3. **Custom branded link (Rebrandly):**
   - Go to [rebrandly.com](https://rebrandly.com)
